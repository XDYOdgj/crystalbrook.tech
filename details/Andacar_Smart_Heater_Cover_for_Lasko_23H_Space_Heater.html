The Andacar Smart Heater Cover

Keep your Lasko 23"H space heater looking brand new all year round with the Andacar Smart Heater Cover. This essential accessory is crafted from high-quality tear-resistant oxford fabric, ensuring long-lasting durability and ease of maintenance. It provides excellent heat resistance and is fully waterproof, making it perfect for protecting your heater whether you're indoors or outdoors. The breathable design allows it to dry quickly in the presence of wind or sunlight, adding to its versatility and reliability.

Ideal for use around the home, garden, or patio, the Andacar cover offers a dependable shield against dust, snow, and harsh weather conditions. It features a built-in pocket that makes it convenient to store remote controls and other small parts, while the adjustable drawstring ensures a secure and customizable fit. A great choice for anyone seeking a practical, protective solution that keeps their heater clean and ready to use in any climate.
