WXZX Screen Doorway Blanket

This screen doorway blanket combines exceptional thermal insulation and soundproofing to keep your space comfortable throughout the year. Whether you're dealing with chilly winter nights or looking to reduce outside noise, this versatile curtain delivers both warmth and protection.

Features

  • Thickened cotton and PU fabric for superior insulation and soundproofing
  • Transparent PVC window for clear visibility and safety
  • Double-layered and moisture-resistant design for long-lasting durability
  • Easy-to-install velcro system with metal hanging holes
  • Multi-functional use for homes, warehouses, and shops

Why Choose This Product?

This screen doorway blanket is designed to protect your home from the elements while maintaining a sense of openness. With its dual-layer construction, it effectively blocks cold air in winter and stops cool air from escaping during summer. Plus, the built-in soundproofing helps create a peaceful and quiet indoor environment, making it perfect for both residential and commercial use.

Ideal For

This curtain is ideal for homes, greenhouses, hatcheries, warehouses, and shops. It adds a layer of security and comfort without compromising on style or functionality.
